Before you use this app, please read the directions. Do not book a time that has been scheduled by another student; see the table at the bottom of the page.

Directions:



If you click "Book Appointment" and reload the page, then your appointment will show up in the table below. Only I will be able to see your name, email, and topic. You will know that your appointment is confirmed when you see your booking below.

You may only book no less than four hours ahead of your appointment; the app automatically presents only those times. At the end of a day on which there were available times, the app will reset and let you book appointments on that day for the upcoming week. For instance, suppose that today is a Wed the 12th. If you're trying to book Wed the 17th, you'll have to wait until midnight on Thursday the 13th to book. The office hour Zoom link is here.
