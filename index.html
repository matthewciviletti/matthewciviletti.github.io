Welcome to my homepage!

I am a physicist and educator at Queens College, the City University of New York, where I teach physics, astronomy, and cosmology. I am also the Director of the Astronomy Program. I build mathematical models to study several aspects of the universe; these include the universe's very early history, as well as possible solutions to the Fermi paradox. Below I link to my research and all courses I'm currently teaching.
